About us:

The Biggest Little Bean Co. is a privately owned and operated local coffee bar and roaster in Reno, Nv. We strive to offer the best coffee in the region; whether your enjoying a cup with us at one of our several locations or taking home one of our hand-crafted roasts. Our beans are sourced from all over the world from the finest fair-trade farmers. Our most popular current roasts come from Ethiopia and Brazil.

We offer:

Come join us for a drink at one of our several locations. We offer everything from traditional drip coffees to specialized artisan drinks. We will accommodate your preferences and needs with a full suite of dairy-free milks and creams for any of our creamier drinks. Aside from what you expect to find at every coffee bar we also offer innovative ways to brew coffee with methods such as Chemex, Aeropress and Kalita pour-overs.

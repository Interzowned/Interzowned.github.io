About us:

The Biggest Little Bean Co. is a privately owned and operated local coffee raoster and bar in Reno, Nv. We strive to offer the best coffee in the region; wether your enjoying a cup with us at one of our several locations or taking home one of our hand-crafted roasts. Our beans are sourced from all over the world from the finest fairtrade farmers. Our most popular current roasts come from Etheopia and Brazil.

We offer:

  • Dark/medium/light roasted coffee beans
  • Standard drip coffee and iced brews
  • Specialy coffees, ask our baristas
